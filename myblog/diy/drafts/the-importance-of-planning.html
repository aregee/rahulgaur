The Importance Of Planning

Published on January 17, 2011

A failure to plan is a plan to fail.

I consider myself to be a planner. Not a day goes by without me making plans of every sort. From what I should be doing for the day, to what I want or need accomplished by the end of the month. I try to plan out every part of my day, even if they’re just little plans.

Kinds of Plans

“To Do” List

Most of my plans are plain old “To Do” lists. I just list down (in my head or on paper) things I need to do.
